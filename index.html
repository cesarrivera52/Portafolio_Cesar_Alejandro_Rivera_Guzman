Hello There!

Thank you for taking the time to read this section about me. My name is César Alejandro Rivera Guzmán, I am a proud Mexican recient graduate engineer with background as an Industrial with minor in Systems Engineer. Proudly EXATEC from Tecnológico de Monterrey, Campus Puebla, I combine my technical knowledge with creativity, innovation, and discipline to bring ideas to life.

From an early age, I have been passionate about understanding and improving processes.
I believe that technology, strategic thinking, and adaptability are key to transforming challenges into opportunities .

My academic journey has led me to work in different areas aiming to create sustainable and scalable results such as:

  • Data analysis and metaheuristics models
  • Electronic manufacturing and process optimization
  • Software development and web applications
  • Interactive dashboards and decision-making tools
  • Social impact projects, such as anti-corruption campaigns in Mexico

I also follow a fitness and wellness lifestyle, deeply inspired by a discipline philosophy. This mindset helps me stay focused, resilient, and consistent both in my personal life and professional projects. I believe in continuous growth and building habits that lead to long-term impact.

Books that have shaped my way of thinking and that I highly recommend include:

  • The Four Agreements (Spanish Version) by Don Miguel Ruiz

  • Outliers: The Story of Success by Malcom Gladwell

  • Make your Bed Little Things by Admiral William H. McRaven
