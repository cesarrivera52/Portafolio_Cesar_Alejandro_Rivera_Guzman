Inventory Optimization & Process Implementation

Scrum Master | Agile Implementation & Operational Alignment

February – March 2025

Situation

The inventory counting process was manual, inconsistent across operational shifts, and lacked standardized documentation. This limited visibility, generated reporting delays, and reduced process control.

Task

Lead the digital transformation of the inventory process to improve accuracy, standardize workflows, and enhance cross-team coordination.

Action

Formalized the project using ProjectLibre to define scope, timeline, milestones, and resource allocation. Developed a structured Project Charter and implemented Agile coordination with sprint planning and reviews.

Results

Delivered a scalable digital inventory solution that improved transparency, strengthened operational control, and enhanced reporting consistency.

Tools

  • ProjectLibre
  • Kanban Board
  • Microsoft Teams
  • Azure DevOps
  • Scrum Framework
